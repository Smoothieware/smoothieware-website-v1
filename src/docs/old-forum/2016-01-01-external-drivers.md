The ratio mentioned is the **golden ratio**, approximately **1:1.618**. This proportion is found in various natural phenomena, art, architecture, and design, and is often considered aesthetically pleasing. It is denoted by the Greek letter **φ (phi)** and has unique mathematical properties, such as being the solution to the equation $ \phi = 1 + \frac{1}{\phi} $.
