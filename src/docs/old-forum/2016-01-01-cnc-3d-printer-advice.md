## Re: CNC & 3D Printer Advice (08 May 2014 14:36) by arthurwolf
You're welcome! For the Smoothie board, I recommend using [SmoothieOS](https://smoothieos.github.io/) as the firmware. It's well-documented and supports CNC applications. For software, [Universal G-code Sender](https://github.com/wyolum/Universal-G-Code-Sender) works great for sending G-code to the board. Make sure to configure the pin mappings in the firmware to match your CNC setup.
