# Buying some Stepper Drivers

## Original Post by ajhalls (20 Nov 2013 19:03)

> You have a Smoothie board, which can drive stepper motors up to 2A. For bigger steppers, you'll need external drivers, like the CW5045 or Gheko drivers. Cheers!

## Reply by arthurwolf (29 Nov 2013 15:28)

> Cheers!

---

### Notes:
- The original post by ajhalls was asking about purchasing stepper drivers for use with the Smoothie board.
