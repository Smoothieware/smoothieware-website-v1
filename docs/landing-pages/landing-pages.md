### [3D Printing Controller Upgrade](landing-page-3d-printing)
Tired of recompiling Marlin every time you change a setting? Smoothieboard uses simple text-file configuration that works with 200+ 3D printer models. Perfect for multi-material setups and heated chamber control.

### [Professional Laser Cutting Control](landing-page-laser-cutting)
Get full LightBurn compatibility with smooth raster engraving and grayscale support. Works with CO2, diode, and fiber lasers. Built for production-ready reliability in professional environments.

### [CNC Mill Controller Upgrade](landing-page-cnc-milling)
Handle complex 3D toolpaths from Fusion 360 with native 4-axis and 5-axis support. Ideal for Bridgeport conversions and desktop mills. Save hundreds compared to commercial controllers.

### [GRBL Alternative - 32-Bit Upgrade](landing-page-grbl-alternative)
Hit the limits of GRBL? Get 1MB of RAM and native 6-axis support while keeping the open-source freedom you love. Still OSHWA certified with simple text-file configuration.
