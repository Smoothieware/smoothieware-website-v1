## What's New

This draft incorporates comprehensive information extracted from professional laser cutter manuals, covering:

- **Enhanced Safety Information**: Class IV laser hazards, prohibited materials with chemistry explanations, fire prevention, electrical safety
- **Laser PSU Wiring**: Complete H/L/P/G/IN/5V pinout explanations and TTL vs PWM distinction
- **Optical System**: CO2 tube fundamentals, 3-mirror beam path, focus lens types
- **Water Cooling**: Why distilled water is mandatory, flow/temperature requirements, troubleshooting
- **Air Assist**: Purpose, installation, benefits
- **Maintenance Procedures**: Optical cleaning, mirror alignment, belt tension, lubrication schedules
- **Operating Procedures**: Pre-operation checklists, test cutting methodology, monitoring, post-operation care
- **Best Practices**: Cut quality optimization, engraving techniques, design for laser cutting
- **Systematic Calibration**: 3x3 grid tests, power/speed grids, progressive testing
- **Material Safety**: Safe/prohibited materials, preparation, fixturing
- **Troubleshooting**: Systematic diagnostic methodology, symptom categories, common solutions
- **EMI Protection**: Ferrite bead placement, cable routing best practices
