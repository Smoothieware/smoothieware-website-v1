# {::nomarkdown}<gcode>G11</gcode>{:/nomarkdown} G-code

{::nomarkdown}<gcode>G11</gcode>{:/nomarkdown} is the opposite of {::nomarkdown}<gcode>G10</gcode>{:/nomarkdown}, it means "unretract" instead of "retract".

This G-code is used to reverse the filament retraction performed by {::nomarkdown}<gcode>G10</gcode>{:/nomarkdown}, pushing the filament back to its normal position for printing.

{::nomarkdown}
<sl-alert variant="neutral" open>
  <sl-icon slot="icon" name="info-circle"></sl-icon>

</sl-alert>
{:/nomarkdown}
