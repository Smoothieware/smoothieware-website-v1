# <gcode>G11</gcode> G-code

<gcode>G11</gcode> is the opposite of <gcode>G10</gcode>, it means "unretract" instead of "retract".

This G-code is used to reverse the filament retraction performed by <gcode>G10</gcode>, pushing the filament back to its normal position for printing.
{:/nomarkdown}
